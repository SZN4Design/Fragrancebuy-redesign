Questions, answered

The things first-time shoppers ask most — cleared up before you check out.

Yes — 100%. Every fragrance is sourced from trusted suppliers, checked on arrival, and we never sell imitations or knock-offs. If a listing is anything other than brand-new boxed, the label tells you exactly what it is.
A tester is the identical fragrance a brand sends to retail counters, in plain packaging instead of a retail box. Same juice, same performance, lower price — it simply may arrive with a plain cap or box.
A decant is a smaller amount of the genuine fragrance transferred into a travel atomizer. It’s the low-risk way to live with a scent for a few weeks before committing to a full bottle.
Boxed items arrive factory-sealed. Testers, decants and pre-owned items aren’t sealed by nature — but every listing clearly states the condition and, for pre-owned, shows the fill level.
Most orders dispatch within 1–2 business days from Canada, with delivery typically in 2–7 business days depending on your province. Every order ships with tracking.
Eligible items can be returned within 30 days. Because fragrance is personal, some conditions apply (for example, unused and in original condition) — the full policy is linked at checkout.
Start with our Gift-safe picks or the Scent Finder, lean toward crowd-pleasing fresh or warm scents, and consider a gift set for instant wow. Not sure? A decant or smaller size is a thoughtful, low-pressure choice.
